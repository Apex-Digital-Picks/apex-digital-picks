Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Apex Digital Picks ("we", "us") builds AI-powered business communication tools, including ApexFlow, a WhatsApp assistant used by businesses to respond to their customers. This policy explains what personal information we process, why, and your rights under the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA).

1. Information we collect

When you interact with a WhatsApp assistant powered by ApexFlow, we process:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and profile name
  • The content of messages you send to the business, including voice notes
  • Message timestamps and delivery status
  • Information you choose to share for quotes or bookings, such as your name and order details

We do not collect information beyond what you send in the conversation.

2. How we use it

  • To generate draft replies to your questions, which are reviewed and approved by a human at the business before sending
  • To prepare quotes and documents you request
  • To keep a record of the conversation so the business can assist you

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use your conversations to send marketing unless you have expressly opted in. You can opt out of any broadcast messages at any time by replying STOP.

3. Who processes your information

Messages are transmitted through the WhatsApp Business Platform operated by Meta Platforms, Inc. Draft replies are generated using AI services provided by Anthropic. Conversation records are stored on servers we control, hosted by Hetzner Online GmbH. Each provider processes data only as needed to deliver the service.

4. Retention

Conversation records are kept for as long as needed to serve you and meet the business's legal obligations, after which they are deleted. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

5. Your rights under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Object to processing or withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa) at inforegulator.org.za

6. Security

We use encrypted connections, access controls, and human review gates to protect your information. Only authorised people at the relevant business can view conversations.
